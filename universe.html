How to read this visualization?

Though it seems like the possibilities are endless, in reality most cocktails are made using standard ratios and combinations. In this diagram, popular ingredient categories cluster together, showing affinity between groups. Different ingredient categories are encoded using color to more easily show the relationships between them. When you’re planning your next party, it’s just as important to know what not to mix. Zoom in or mouse over the nodes to see how the ingredients interact.
